The Band Members

  • Name: Jennifer Quinn
  • Date of Birth: August 5, 1990
  • Singer & Second Guitarist
Jennifer Quinn is a songwriter, guirtarist, and pianist, whose passion for music started at the early age of 7. After graduating from Townsend Harris High School in New York, Jennifer enrolled at Rutgers University where she double majored in composition and jazz studies. After college, she joined a jazz rock band and became involved with a variety of musical projects. When the jazz rock group disbanded, Jennifer decided to round up two close friends and start her own indie rock band.
  • Name: Nana Miller
  • Date of Birth: June 27, 1991
  • First Guitarist
The youngest member of the group, Nana Miller did not discover her talent for the guitar until she was 22-years-old. Though always having a passion for music, Nana's interest had always been in classical music. At Cardinal Spellman High, a private school, Nana played the cello in the school orchestra. It was after being accepted into NYU, and aiming to minor in the universities' Music Technology program, did Nana start toying with the electric guitar.
  • Name: Holly Campbell
  • Date of Birth: Jannuary 13, 1988
  • Drummer
Holly Campbell grew up in the small town of Lititz, Pennsylvannia. Already knowing how to play the piano and winning several junior competitions, after middle school Holly's family moved down to New York. She began attending Townsend Harris High School, where she became friends with Jennifer Quinn. During the time, Holly started to take less of an interest in the piano and more of an interest in the drums. Self-taught, Holly kept her drumming as a hobby until joining La La Land.
