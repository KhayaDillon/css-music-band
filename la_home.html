LA LA LAND is an up and coming, indie rock band. In just a year, the group gained a million views on YouTube, came in second in the New York State Field Band competition, and has come out with it's second album, COLORLESS. With only three members, Jennifer, Nana, and Holly, this all girl group showcases a unique dynamic. Along with it's soaring vocals and signature dream-like sound, LA LA LAND is starting to make their name. The band has won over fans and critics with it's unique electronic rock, dream pop, and R&B hybrid sound. Born and bred in New York, this local band has begun is now preparing for its first tour.
